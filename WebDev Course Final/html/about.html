About Me

This is who I am.

Earlier this year, I was married to the most wonderful woman in the world, Miranda. She and I have been enjoying our first year of marriage together as we have moved around the country, gone on a multitude of fun trips, and have both learned that marriage is not always as easy as we first thought. Our first apartment together was a retrofitted garage with very little insulation, so the winter was rather chilly. We moved to Texas for 6 months to both work as "summer sales bros" for a pest control company. It was one of the most challenging things either of us have ever done, but it definitely brought us closer together. Since then we have been living in Provo, UT where we both attend Brigham Young University.

One of the largest influences in my life has been learning more about the philosophy of stoicism. The author Ryan Holiday has not only published many books about stoicism, but hosts a podcast titled "The Daily Stoic" which helps teach self mastery and emotional maturity. I've learned a lot about how I can better myself, focus on what I truly care about, and maintain more control over how I react to my life events. If you want to learn more about my intellectual side, you can view my resume here.

When I have a little extra free time, I really enjoy being outdoors. Some of the few activities which I am most passionate about are skiing, swimming, and rock climbing. Other hobbies I enjoy include computer programming and UAV piloting, primarily for photography and video purposes.
